!!! info
    Prowlarr is an indexer manager/proxy built on the popular arr .net/reactjs base stack to integrate with your various PVR apps. Prowlarr supports management of both Torrent Trackers and Usenet Indexers. It integrates seamlessly with [LazyLibrarian](https://lazylibrarian.gitlab.io/), [Lidarr](lidarr.md), [Mylar3](https://github.com/mylar3/mylar3), [Radarr](radarr.md), and [Sonarr](sonarr.md) offering complete management of your indexers with no per app Indexer setup required (we do it all).
